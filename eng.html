About us

CN Finanzas connects its clients, from the private and public sector, with the most complete network of financial information on Argentina’s public entities (national, provincial, municipal and other entities).

We create value through tools that allow decision makers to have more accuracy, confidence and swiftness. Our experience in the area and the continuous expansion of our information sources allows CN Finanzas to become and essential component when it comes down to sub-sovereign finances.
